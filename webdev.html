How to Become a Web Developer

Have a Goal
Become a Web Developer for Google
    SMART
  1. Specific
  2. Measurable
  3. Attainable
  4. Realistic
  5. Time Specific

My Personal goal is to become a sucessful Web Devoloper for a major sports team and to be able to help others to be able to reach their goals..

When you have a goal in mind it is important to follow the SMART method or you will probably be like many others that never accomplish them.

Start with the Basics
Google is your Friend
  1. Learn HTML
  2. Learn CSS
  3. Learn Javascript
  4. BGoogle what you don't know.
  5. Don't be affraid to ask for help

As you begin your coding journey it can be scary to know where to start. It is important to start with the basics. That means to first to become familiar with HTML and CSS. After you have become comfortable you can learn to give it functionality with Javascript. After you have learned those you will be able to create almost anything and will be able to learn other languages much more easily.

One great thing about coding is the great community their is and so many resources to help you at all times. Some of these things are Google, your friends and coworkers, as well as Free Code Camp and much more.

Create a Workspace
Call it a Coding Cave
  • Have a Modern Laptop
  • Have a Notepad and Pen
  • Have a Whiteboard
  • Have a Comfortable Office Chair
  • Have a Relaxing Playlist

As an aspiring coder it is important to have a place set that you can be alone away from distractions to do your studies and your work. It is also important to have a good laptop/computer that meets technology requirements. My personal preferance is a Macbook Pro. It is also important to have many office supplies to take good notes to help you to learn and to write what you want to build. Two of my favorite things are a nice office chair that helps you to stay working for hours comfortably and a good playis that doesn't distract you.

Take Breaks
Don't Work Too Hard

As you code sometimes you will have so much fun that you lose track of time or you might get stuck with an issue. In both situations it is important to take a break. This might be a quick break just to walk around or a longer break to excercise or to do a hobby.

Showcase Your Work
Build a Portfolio

As you have built different projects it is important to show them off. This can be mulitple ways such as uploading them to Github, Heroku, or by making a personal website. This is extremely important because employers or customers would like to see what you have done to know what you can do.

Another way you can share your work is through Social Media by connecting with others to build your coding community. It is a good way to find job referals.
